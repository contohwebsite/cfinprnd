Welcome to the R&DCINP

Research and Development Center for Indonesian Natural Products

Welcome to the Research and Development Center for Indonesian Natural Products (R&DCINP). The center was founded on 20 May 2020 under Vidya Mandhala Foundation, Act No. AHU-0003272.AH.01.04.2016 dated 20-01-2016 by Ministry of Law and Human Right, Republic of Indonesia. The R&DCINP consists of two divisions, Research Division and Development Division, to search, develop and commercialize potentially useful Indonesian natural products. The center is academic research and development entity devoted to the discovery and development of fragrance, pharmaceutical products and natural bio-pesticides based on the amazing diversity of tropical Indonesian natural products.
